Achievements

It is with great pleasure we welcome you to the Rustomjee Cambridge International School and Junior College, an initiative of the Rustomjee Group to set benchmarks in the field of education. The Rustomjee Group has achieved an enviable reputation in the field of real estate development, construction management, and education. Our philosophy is “To Make Education a Priority”, and we aim to provide a learning experience that is innovative and fun

Our educational programs reach out to more than 9,500 students, and our expansion plans include establishing education institutions of excellence offering varied courses in vocational and professional development. We have put together a rich resource base of learning experiences to set new standards of excellence in K-12 education. This program is designed to meet the diverse educational needs of young achievers. The learning culture values individual vision, intellectual discipline and teamwork. Our success is demonstrated in the achievements of our students in academics, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. By the time participants complete this program they will have undergone a significant personal and professional transformation preparing them for future career success. The program structure is uniquely designed to promote excellence in students looking to immerse themselves in the learning process. This rigorous curriculum follows a proven progression of coursework in essential core requirements, and an enriching selection of concentrated assignments on and off campus.
